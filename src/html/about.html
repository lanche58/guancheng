甲冠天下  众志成城

Become the Pioneer of Education·Cultivate Talents for the Future

冠城集团于1992年从香港进入内地投资发展,系首批参与西部大开发的侨资企业。现已发展成集教育产业、地产开发、物业管理、金融投资、生态农业为一体的多元化集团企业。


集团始终秉持“诚信为本、社会利益至上”的企业宗旨，坚持“用专注成就价值、用专业铸造品质”的经营理念，以“人品优先、能力为重、团队为王、创变共赢”为人才理念，以“文化内涵”、“文化渗透”和“文化力量”来引领全体冠城人的思想、道德和价值观，形成了强大的凝聚力、创造力和生命力。


集团将以创新驱动为核心，以品质取胜为生命，执着坚毅与审时度势，优化布局与整合资源，大力推进现代企业运营管理机制建设，倾力构筑人才高地，强势打造核心竞争力，形成以文化教育为先导，多支柱、复合型、具有核心优势的集团化企业。


Guancheng group entered the Mainland from Hongkong in 1992, as the first overseas Chinese venture taking part in the great western development strategy.Now it has developed into a diversified enterprise which covers education industry, real estate, property management, financial investment and eco-agriculture.


The group always uphold the enterprise purposes of “integrity-based, social interests first”, adhere to the management idea of “realizing the value with concentration, casting the quality with specialty”, and stick to the talent concept of “personality, ability, team spirit and innovation preferred”. It uses “cultural connotation”, “cultural permeation” and “cultural forces” to guide the thinking, morality and the values of all Guancheng people, which has formed strong cohesion, creativity and vitality.


The group will regard innovation-driven as the core, high quality as life and death. It will be firm and persistent, optimizing layout and integrating resources, giving great impetus to establish modern enterprise operation mechanism. It will build human resource highland, construct core competence with great efforts, in the hope of being a compound enterprise group with multi-pillar and core advantages, taking cultural education as the forerunner.
